Time management · Corporate delay

Same idea as traffic or a late flight: pick any corporate and the earliest hour they can take a meeting (slot start time). Until then they are blocked; the grid reflows for everyone else. Defaults match the brief example: Toyota Next from 13:00.

Official rules

Sector match: startups only meet corporates in the same sector. No collisions: a corporate cannot appear in both rooms in the same hour. Capacity: each startup gets at least one 1-hour meeting when the sector graph allows it. Corporate delay: optionally block one corporate before a chosen hour (e.g. traffic).
