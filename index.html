Event & roster

Event setup

Day window, meeting length, and how many meetings can run at once (rooms / tables). Max 24 time slots × 20 rooms.

Shown in the event picker and the calendar title.

Same as “tables”: one column per room in the grid (1–20).

Roster

CSV, JSON, or Excel (.xlsx / .xls): first sheet, columns name, type (Corp or Startup), sector. Max 20 per side. JSON or Excel may include optional id per row.

Participants

Rename, re-sector, add, or remove corporates and startups. Use Generate schedule below to refresh the grid.

Pinned meetings (human in the loop)

Lock in who meets whom (e.g. Toyota Next with QuickCharge). You only pick the pair; time and room are assigned by the tool. Other empty slots are filled as usual. Pinned pairs must share a sector.

Time management · Corporate delay

Pick a corporate and the earliest clock time their meetings may start (compared to each slot’s start). Until then they are blocked.

Official rules

Sector match: startups only meet corporates in the same sector. No collisions: the same corporate or startup cannot appear in two tables in the same time slot. Capacity: each startup gets at least one meeting in an open slot when the sector graph allows it. Pinned pairs are placed first. Corporate delay: optionally block one corporate before a chosen time.

Changes save in the background; the calendar updates when you generate.
